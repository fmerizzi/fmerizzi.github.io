<section id="about" class="content-section">
  <h2><span class="tag2">about</span></h2>
  <p>I am a researcher working on deep learning for meteorology. After completing my PhD at the University of Bologna, I moved into industry research, where I design neural models for weather-related applications. My background is in scientific machine learning, with a focus on generative models.</p>
  <p>Check out my wind forecasts with diffusion models at: <a href=" https://fasterweather.com/"> fasterweather.com</a>
